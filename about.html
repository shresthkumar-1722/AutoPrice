About AutoPrice

AutoPrice is a Regional Car Valuation System designed to help users compare Ex-Showroom and On-Road prices of popular cars across different cities in India.

Our Objective:

The main objective of AutoPrice is to provide users with accurate and easy-to-understand car pricing information based on their selected city and brand.

Features:

  • ✔ Compare car prices across multiple cities.
  • ✔ Explore popular car brands and models.
  • ✔ View different variants of each car.
  • ✔ Display Ex-Showroom and On-Road prices.
  • ✔ Easy-to-use and responsive interface.

Technologies Used:
